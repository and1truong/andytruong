Listening
====

## [14 Tips](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OualsHB1FqE)

1. Write no more than 3 words.
2. Get used to Bristish English (BBC)
3. Bristish vocab (lorry vs. truck)
4. Spelling counts
5. cat vs cats
6. Practice tests
7. Prediction (the woman will travel on _______)
